## 🏢 About the Company

AtliQ Hardwares is a global computer hardware manufacturer that sells products
through channels like Amazon, Croma, and Best Buy. As the business scaled,
complete reliance on Excel became a critical liability — planning files grew
unmanageable, and a catastrophic crash forced a complete operational rethink.

AtliQ responded by migrating to a structured **MySQL database** and onboarding
a dedicated data analytics team to deliver timely, reliable business insights.

---

## 🔍 Problem Statement

The data team regularly receives ad-hoc requests from stakeholders to analyze:
- Sales trends across markets and time periods
- Product demand and inventory performance
- Channel-level and customer-level revenue breakdowns
- Forecast accuracy and supply chain efficiency

Previously, these questions were answered manually in Excel — slow, error-prone,
and not scalable. This project builds a **centralized, SQL-based analytical
solution** to respond to these business questions quickly and consistently.
